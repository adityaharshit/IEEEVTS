About Bangalore Section

IEEE Bangalore Section was established in 1977 and is one of the most vibrant and dynamic IEEE Section across globe. Over the years the strength of the section has grown steadily and currently the Section membership stands more than 8600. This membership is spread across various sectors and is a nice blend of Industry, Academia and Research organizations…
